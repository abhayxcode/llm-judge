# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be kind, be patient, assume good intent, give and accept constructive feedback. Harassment, personal attacks, and discrimination are not tolerated.

## Reporting

Report violations privately to `abhayxcode@gmail.com`. Reports are handled confidentially.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may warn, temporarily ban, or permanently ban contributors who violate this code. Decisions are at maintainer discretion.
